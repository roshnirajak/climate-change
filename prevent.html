Can we Stop Climate Change?

Yes, we can...

For taking action:

  • Satellites are valuable tools we can use to increase our knowledge of climate processes and their impacts,
    and thus properly target our action plans and response activities
    Here are some sattelites used for observation:
    RADARSAT Constellation Mission and RADARSAT-2, SCISAT, MOPITT (on Terra), OSIRIS (on Odin), CloudSat

  • What do Sattelites help us with
    • Assess the state of the ozone layer in order to continue protecting it
    • Monitor air quality and measure pollution
    • Track changes in ice and permafrost conditions
    • Monitor soil moisture on a global scale
  • Also at an individual level we can do so many things like reduce waste and use renewable energy
